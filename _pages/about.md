I am a theoretical physicist working on strong gravitational fields and exploring alternatives to Einstein's General Relativity. Currently I am a postdoctoral researcher at INFN Roma 1 and Sapienza University of Rome, where I investigate the dynamics of compact objects and gravitational-wave signals in the strong-field regime. My work aims to test General Relativity and its alternatives using observations from gravitational-wave detectors. This simple page serves as a starting point for my online presence.
