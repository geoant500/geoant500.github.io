Georgios Antoniou

I am a postdoctoral researcher at INFN Rome and Sapienza University of Rome, working on strong gravitational physics and exploring alternatives to General Relativity. My work aims to test gravity theories using observations of compact objects and gravitational waves.

I use a range of theoretical and computational techniques to investigate the dynamics of compact objects and gravitational-wave signals. You can learn more about my research, publications and projects on this site.
